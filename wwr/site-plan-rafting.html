Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to establish brand awareness in the adventure tourism space, and drive booking sales.

Audience

The target audience for our rafting website are 18-60 years old, middle income or higher, and love adventuring in the outdoors. They want a new experience with all safety measures adhered to. On our site they want to find all the information they need to understand the trip, trip safety, how to book, what to bring, and how to get here.

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on)

Our target customers are people aged 18-60, middle income or higher, who enjoy adventure travel and tourism such as nature-based activities, camping, hiking, skiing/snowboarding, surfing, horse-back riding, and the like.

What do they want?

Our customers want a new experience, disconnected from the world, that provides anywhere from a small to large amount of adrenaline with all safety guidelines adhered to.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

Our website should address the location of the trip, itinerary, weather, what to expect, clothing required, level of fitness required, price, how to book, and how to get to our locations.
